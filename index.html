Our Story

The art of the cap,
elevated to luxury

Executive Cap was born from a simple conviction: prestige accessories should not be reserved for the great fashion houses alone. Inspired by the iconography of the White House and American power, we created a line of caps that embodies silent authority.

Each piece is conceived in our Washington atelier by craftsmen selected for their mastery. Materials are sourced from the world's finest mills. Nothing is left to chance.

"Power is not proclaimed. It is worn."
— Executive Cap, Founding Manifesto, 2024
Our Craft

Six hours of work
per piece

Our manufacturing process is uncompromising: material selection, millimeter-precise cutting, hand embroidery, individual quality control. Every cap leaves the atelier with a unique serial number engraved inside.

The Gold edition benefits from an additional protocol: the application of genuine 18-karat gold thread is carried out by a master embroiderer whose initials appear on the inside of each piece.
